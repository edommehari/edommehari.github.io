About Me

Defining Narratives and Building Worlds.

I have built a dynamic career driving product operations and community strategy for major tech launches at Google. Navigating high-stakes environments, I’ve learned how to execute with precision while fostering genuine collaboration across teams.

But my journey didn't start in the boardroom. As a former performing artist who has released albums and played live concerts, my foundation has always been deeply creative. I'm also the creator of hand-tufted rugs, an active public speaker, and an emerging author currently crafting my debut novel.

I thrive at this intersection of structure and art. My true passion lies in providing actionable advice, sparking meaningful debates, and motivating others to completely define their own narratives and build their own worlds.

The Art of the Pivot

Motivating Yourself Through Creative Transitions

Overcome the fear of leaving one established identity to build a completely new one. Drawing on my transition from performing artist to emerging author and tech operator, this session provides a tangible roadmap for reinventing yourself fearlessly.

Lighting the Spark

The Hustle Behind the Hobbies

Take the decisive leap from ideation to physical creation. We break down the sheer logistics and mindset needed to manage multiple entrepreneurial pursuits—balancing a rigorous tech career while producing music, generating content, creating tufted rugs, and public speaking.

The Collaborative Catalyst

Leveraging Your Network to Scale Your Vision

Explore how to build and leverage authentic relationships to elevate your projects. Learn how to turn casual connections into powerful partnerships, shaped by my experiences driving cross-functional alignment in high-stakes tech launches.

The Butterfly Effect of Taking a Chance

Witness how micro-decisions and calculated risks compound over time to shape your entire career and personal brand. Inspired by the narrative design of crafting a novel, this session explores the real-life butterfly effect of entrepreneurship and betting on yourself.
